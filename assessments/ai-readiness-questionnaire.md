# AI Readiness Questionnaire

This questionnaire helps organizations evaluate their readiness to adopt artificial intelligence for productivity, automation, and decision support.

Rate each area from 1 to 5.

- 1 = Not prepared
- 2 = Limited readiness
- 3 = Moderate readiness
- 4 = Strong readiness
- 5 = Fully prepared

## Scoring Interpretation

### 6–12
Early-stage readiness. Focus on policy, awareness, and identifying low-risk pilot use cases.

### 13–20
Moderate readiness. Focus on workflow selection, governance, and initial AI pilots.

### 21–30
Strong readiness. Organization is positioned to scale AI productivity initiatives responsibly.

---

## Recommended Output

At the conclusion of this questionnaire, organizations should receive:

- overall readiness score
- key strengths
- primary gaps
- recommended pilot use cases
- governance priorities

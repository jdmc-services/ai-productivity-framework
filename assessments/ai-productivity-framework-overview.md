# AI Productivity Framework

## Overview

The AI Productivity Framework provides organizations with a structured approach to adopting artificial intelligence to improve operational efficiency and decision support.

This repository includes assessment tools, prompt libraries, and governance templates designed to help organizations deploy AI responsibly and effectively.

---

## Framework Components

### AI Readiness Assessment

Evaluates an organization's readiness for AI adoption by examining leadership alignment, infrastructure capability, data availability, and governance maturity.

### Productivity Prompt Library

Provides reusable prompts that improve productivity across meetings, reporting, documentation, and operational workflows.

### AI Governance Policy

Establishes responsible usage guidelines to ensure AI tools are used securely, ethically, and in compliance with organizational policies.

---

## Implementation Model

### Phase 1 – Assessment
Evaluate organizational readiness and identify AI opportunities.

### Phase 2 – Pilot
Deploy AI tools in targeted workflows such as documentation, reporting, and knowledge retrieval.

### Phase 3 – Integration
Integrate AI into operational processes with governance and monitoring.

---

## Objective

The goal of the AI Productivity Framework is to help organizations:

• improve operational efficiency  
• reduce manual administrative work  
• enhance decision-making  
• adopt AI responsibly

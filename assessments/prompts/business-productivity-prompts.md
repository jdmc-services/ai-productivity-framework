# Business Productivity Prompt Library

Reusable prompts designed to improve workplace productivity using AI.

---

## Meeting Summary

Summarize the following meeting transcript.

Extract:
- key decisions
- action items
- responsible individuals
- deadlines

Provide the output as a concise executive summary.

---

## Email Drafting

Draft a professional email explaining the following issue to stakeholders.

Ensure the tone is professional, concise, and solution-oriented.

---

## Policy Drafting

Draft an organizational policy for the following scenario.

Ensure the language is clear, professional, and suitable for executive review.

---

## Document Simplification

Rewrite the following technical document so that non-technical leadership can understand it.

Focus on clarity, business impact, and risk considerations.

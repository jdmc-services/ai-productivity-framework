# Business Productivity Prompt Library

This library contains reusable AI prompts designed to improve workplace productivity.

## Example Prompts

### Meeting Summary

Summarize the following meeting transcript and extract key decisions, action items, and responsible parties.

### Policy Drafting

Draft a professional organizational policy for the following scenario. Ensure the language is clear, compliant, and suitable for executive review.

### Operational Analysis

Analyze the following operational data and identify key insights, risks, and recommended actions.

### Executive Brief

Create a one-page executive summary explaining the following technical concept in clear business language.

# AI Acceptable Use Policy (Starter Template)

This document provides baseline guidance for responsible AI use inside an organization.

## Principles

1. AI must support human decision making, not replace accountability.
2. Sensitive data must not be entered into public AI tools without approval.
3. AI-generated outputs must be reviewed before operational use.

## Governance Areas

### Data Protection
Ensure confidential information is not exposed through AI systems.

### Human Oversight
All AI-generated outputs must be reviewed by responsible personnel.

### Transparency
Employees must disclose when AI is used to generate reports, analysis, or communications.

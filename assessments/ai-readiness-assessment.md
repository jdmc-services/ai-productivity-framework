# AI Readiness Assessment

This assessment evaluates whether an organization is prepared to adopt AI productivity solutions.

## Assessment Areas

### Data Availability
Determine whether operational data exists that can support AI-assisted workflows.

### Workflow Maturity
Evaluate how structured and repeatable operational processes are.

### Security and Compliance
Identify potential security and regulatory risks associated with AI adoption.

### Automation Potential
Identify repetitive tasks that could benefit from automation or AI assistance.

### Organizational Readiness
Evaluate leadership alignment, governance capability, and workforce readiness.
